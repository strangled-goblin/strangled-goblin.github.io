NPCs:The Ughr

Overview

The Ughr are a race of reptilian humanoids - somewhat similar to the Gell encountered on Kebora, though considerably less hostile.

Encounters with the party

☇ The Goblin Halls of Qalin

While exploring a Dwarven ruin on the strange island of Qalin, the party noticed a an Ughr watching them closely. After following the creature back to it's small tribe, the trio convinced the leader, Ughr-eshi, that they were peaceful.

Notable NPCs

☇ Ughr-eshi

A larger and seemingly more inteligent member of the Ughr tribe encountered on Qalin, Ughr-eshi appears to serve as their leader.
