NPCs:The Gell

Overview

The Gell are a race of reptilian humanoids - somewhat similar to the Ughr encountered briefly on the island of Qalin, though considerably more hostile.

Encounters with the party

☇ An Elvish delegation in the forest

Sour, a squad mage from the Fourth, asked the party to investigate an area of null magic in a nearby forest. Tracking the signs of a struggle, the trio assisted a group of half-elves in defeating a hunting party of Gell, as well as the Hill Giant Klagg. The elf prince Rudzi was part of the elvish group, who were heading North for a treaty signing. While helping treat their injured, Harren was surpised at the firm refusal of his magical healing, but respected the request of the elves.
