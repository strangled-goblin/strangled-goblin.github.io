NPCs:Vaetr - The Wandering Empire

Overview

Not much is yet known about the Empire of Vaetr, other than that they are not native to the continent of Kabora, having arrived from the North-East. What little exposure the party has had to the Vaetr so far has been limited to a few squad members from "the Fourth", the majority of whom are located North of where the party landed ofter being shipwrecked.

Encounters with the party

☇ The Fourth determines the lay of the land

During a clash with bandits in a beach cave, the party met Dirge, a soldier of the Fourth searching for the remainder of her squad. Upon helping her reunite with Sour and Thumbs, the party learned that the Fourth had landed on Kebora further to the North, and had dispatched several squads secretly to scout the surrounding area.

Notable NPCs

☇ Dirge

First encountered as a prisoner of the bandits, currently Sergeant of her remaining squad.

☇ Sour

Squad mage serving in the Fourth with Dirge and Thumbs. The party helped him track down the source of a local anti-magic field.

☇ Thumbs

Corporal serving in the Fourth with Dirge and Sour.
